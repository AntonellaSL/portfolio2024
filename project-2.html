Universal Basic Income Circles

UI Design

Student Project 2024

Capilano University - IDEA

IXD - 106 User Experience II

Overview

Circles UBI is an app improvement project that aims to improve the user experience and usability for vendors and community members. Through a comprehensive approach focused on simplifying navigation, ensuring consistency in interface design, crafting intuitive user flows and establishing robust feedback and support mechanisms, the project seeks to transform the app into a seamless platform for accessing and managing Universal Basic Income benefits.

Research Statement

To achieve an efficient and frequent use of Circle UBI it is important to improve the usability through intuitive navigation tools such as an organized main menu, a clear profile feature, and color categorizations. These features will motivate users to participate in shopping, exchanging, and consistently using Circles as a primary source of UBI. In addition, users require a prominent call to action strategically positioned at the bottom of the screen within thumb reach. By incorporating this feature, users can quickly and effortlessly transfer Circles, making the whole experience smoother and more inviting.

User Flow

Throughout the project, my focus remained on empowering women through impactful graphics and intuitive design, while adhering to copyright regulations and ethical considerations. The resulting website design for Women Helping Other Woman (WHOW) embodies strength, support, and solidarity, providing a digital platform for women to connect, access resources, and foster positive change.
